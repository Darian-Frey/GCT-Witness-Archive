# Contributing to the Hartley-GCT Archive

We welcome verification, formalization, and extensions of the Hartley Witness proof.

## Areas for Contribution
1. **Formal Verification:** Efforts to port the logic into Lean 4 or Coq.
2. **Computational Scaling:** Running the Macaulay2 scripts for $n > 8$ to further verify stability.
3. **Refinement:** Improving the "Skew-Hook" defense documentation.

## Protocol
All contributions must adhere to the **SCHEMA_V5 Protocol**. Ensure that any updates to the proof logic are reflected in the `REBOOT_KEY.md`.
